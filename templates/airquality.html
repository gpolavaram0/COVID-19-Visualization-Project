Air Quality

Did a decrease in traveling and production affect America's Air Quality?

This page provides heatmaps and charts showing the both the infections and deaths due to COVID-19 in America, and the level of air polutants. Four major pollutants were examined for these visualizations, Surface Ozone (O3), Carbon Monoxide (CO), Nitrogen Dioxide (NO2), and Sulfur Dioxide (SO2).

Data Obtained From:

  • COVID-19 Infections and Deats obtained from Kaggle
  • Air Pollutant data obtained from the AQS API provided by the EPA.

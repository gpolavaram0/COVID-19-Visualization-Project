Data has been pulled from several sources to provide the visualizations given on this website to show the affect of the COVID-19 pandemic on American life. Data sources are given on their respective pages with links for you to further explore yourself.

Due to changes in data collection and reporting for COVID-19 we have taken a "snapshot" of the data sources from January 22nd to May 25th, 2020. Once the data becomes easily accessible again we will reassess updating this website.

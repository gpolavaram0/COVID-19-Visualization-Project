Air Quality Comparison

Explore how four major air quality parameters have changed over the course of the COVID-19 pandemic in America. We've pulled recent data from the Environmental Protection Agency's API which provides data on every air quality parameter there is. For the purposes of these visualizations we pulled the levels of Carbon Monoxide, Ozone, Nitrogen Dioxide, and Sulfur Dioxide levels in the air over America since January 22, 2020. Due to the struggles of COVID and the nature of pulling this kind of data currently March 31, 2020 is the most recent data available.

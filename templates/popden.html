Population Density

How did states' population density affect the spread of COVID?

This page provides both a daily spread comparison as well as a timeline comparison for the spread of COVID-19 vs. the population density in each state. Viral infections spread quicker in more densely populated areas due to higher rates of contact with infected individuals.

Data Obtained From:

  • COVID-19 Infections and Deats obtained from Kaggle
  • Population data obtained from the 2010 Census conducted by the Census Bureau. GeoJson format provided by Leaflet Tutorials
